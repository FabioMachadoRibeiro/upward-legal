Privacy Policy - UPWARD Legal

Last Updated: December 2024

1. Introduction

UPWARD Legal ("we", "our" or "company") is committed to protecting the privacy and personal data of our clients, users, and visitors. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information in compliance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), and other applicable data protection laws.

2. Definitions

For the purposes of this policy:

  • Personal Data: any information relating to an identified or identifiable natural person.
  • Data Subject: the natural person to whom the personal data relates.
  • Data Controller: UPWARD Legal, the legal entity responsible for decisions regarding the processing of personal data.
  • Processing: any operation performed on personal data (collection, storage, use, sharing, etc.).

3. Data Collected

We may collect the following categories of personal data:

3.1 Identification Data

  • Full name
  • Tax identification number (SSN, EIN, etc.)
  • Government-issued ID
  • Date of birth
  • Nationality

3.2 Contact Data

  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Residential or business address

3.3 Professional Data

  • Job title or position
  • Employer
  • Professional license number (BAR, etc.)

3.4 Navigation Data

  • IP address
  • Browser type
  • Operating system
  • Pages visited
  • Time spent
  • Cookies and similar technologies

3.5 Sensitive Data

In specific cases related to the legal services provided, we may process sensitive data with express consent or legal obligation, including:

  • Health data (when necessary for specific cases)
  • Biometric data
  • Information about legal proceedings

4. Purposes of Processing

We use your personal data for the following purposes:

4.1 Provision of Legal Services

  • Legal consulting and advisory
  • Drafting contracts and documents
  • Representation in legal and administrative proceedings
  • Case and process management

4.2 Communication

  • Sending communications about our services
  • Responding to requests and inquiries
  • Case update notifications
  • Newsletter and informational materials (with consent)

4.3 Legal Compliance

  • Responding to requests from competent authorities
  • Fulfilling tax and accounting obligations
  • Defense in legal or administrative proceedings

4.4 Service Improvement

  • Analysis of digital channel usage
  • Development of new services
  • Personalization of user experience

4.5 Security

  • Fraud prevention
  • Protection against cyber attacks
  • Ensuring information security

5. Legal Basis for Processing

The processing of your personal data is based on the following legal grounds:

  • Consent: when you expressly authorize the processing
  • Legal or regulatory compliance: when necessary to comply with the law
  • Contract performance: for the provision of contracted services
  • Legitimate interests: for legitimate purposes of UPWARD Legal
  • Vital interests: when necessary to protect life or physical integrity
  • Public interest: when processing is in the public interest
  • Legal claims: for the establishment, exercise, or defense of legal claims

6. Data Sharing

Your personal data may be shared with:

6.1 Service Providers

  • Cloud hosting and storage services
  • Case management tools
  • Email and communication services
  • Data analytics platforms

6.2 Public Authorities

  • Courts and judicial bodies
  • Regulatory and oversight agencies
  • Law enforcement authorities (when legally required)
  • Tax authorities

6.3 Business Partners

  • Legal correspondents
  • Associate attorneys
  • Expert witnesses and technical specialists

6.4 Third Parties in Corporate Transactions

In the event of a merger, acquisition, reorganization, or asset sale, your data may be transferred to successors.

Important: We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes without your express consent.

7. Storage and Security

7.1 Security Measures

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal data against unauthorized access, loss, destruction, or alteration, including:

  • Encryption of data in transit and at rest
  • Role-based access controls
  • Multi-factor authentication
  • Continuous security monitoring
  • Regular backups
  • Team training in data protection

7.2 Retention Period

Your personal data will be stored for the period necessary to:

  • Fulfill the purposes described in this policy
  • Comply with legal obligations (e.g., statute of limitations periods)
  • Exercise the company's legal rights
  • Preserve documents required by professional regulations

After the end of these periods, data will be securely deleted or anonymized.

7.3 International Transfer

If international data transfer is necessary, we will ensure that:

  • The destination country has adequate data protection legislation
  • There are specific contractual protection clauses
  • You have been informed and consented to the transfer (when applicable)

8. Your Rights as a Data Subject

In accordance with GDPR and CCPA, you have the following rights regarding your personal data:

8.1 Right of Access

Confirm the existence of processing and access your data.

8.2 Right to Rectification

Request correction of incomplete, inaccurate, or outdated data.

8.3 Right to Erasure (Right to be Forgotten)

Request the deletion of unnecessary, excessive, or non-compliant data.

8.4 Right to Data Portability

Request the portability of your data to another service provider.

8.5 Right to Restriction of Processing

Request restriction of data processing in certain circumstances.

8.6 Right to Information

Obtain information about public and private entities with whom we share data.

8.7 Right to Object

Object to processing based on legitimate interests or public interest.

8.8 Right to Withdraw Consent

Withdraw consent at any time.

8.9 Right to Lodge a Complaint

Lodge a complaint with the supervisory authority (ICO or relevant authority).

8.10 Right to Automated Decision Review

Request review of automated decisions.
